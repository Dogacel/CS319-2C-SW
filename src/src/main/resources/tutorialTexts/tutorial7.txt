This is your neighbor's construction zone.

The cards your neighbors built are shown.

You can only see the resources that card gives you.

You can click on their cards to select that resource for trading.

You can click on and trade as many items as you like, as long as you have enough money.

For trading choose one cards, if the user click once, the left sided resource will be traded.
If the user clicks twice, the right-sided resource will be traded.
